ACES Coders v8.0

The ACES Coders v8.0, Sri Lanka’s Biggest Programming Competition took place on 28th February 2020 with the participation of 315 coders for almost all universities and institutions. The competition is an overnight 12-hour Algorithmic Programming contest.

  • Date: 2020-02-28 & 29
  • Organizer: Association of Computer Engineering Students
  • Venue: University of Peradeniya
