TADHACK

When users buying products at most of the supermarkets, they will reward customers for their purchases. Nowadays, the usage of shopping bags become a big problem for the environment. We developed a rewarding system for customers to encourage the use of reusable bags instead of polythene bags. The reward will be given as Mobile Credit/Data which is equal to the saved cost of the polythene bags.

  • Date: 2018-10-13 & 14
  • Organizer: hSenid Mobile - Sri Lanka
  • Venue: Dialog Auditorium, Colombo
