TADHACK

  • Date: 2018-10-13 & 14
  • Organizer: hSenid Mobile - Sri Lanka
  • Venue: Dialog Auditorium, Colombo

TADHack Global is a hackathon that helps people learn about the latest Internet, IT, and Telecoms technologies; and use those technologies to solve problems that matter. This included programmable telecoms (voice, video, messaging), internet of things, cloud computing, robotics, machine learning, speech and face recognition, mobile payments, enterprise communications, decentralized webs, blockchain, encryption, open-source, and many other technologies.

Nowadays, the usage of shopping bags become a big problem for the environment. Besides, when consumers buy products in most supermarkets, they will be rewarding customers for their purchases. Taking this concept into account, we developed a system that benefits consumers by encouraging the use of reusable bags rather than polythene bags. The prize is a mobile loan/data worth the amount saved in polythene bags.
