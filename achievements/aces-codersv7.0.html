ACES Coders v7.0

The ACES Coders v7.0 Sri Lanka’s Biggest Programming Competition took place on 15th December 2018 with the participation of 360 coders for almost all universities and institutions. The competition is an overnight 12-hour Algorithmic Programming contest. Our team was able to achieve 11th place from 120 teams.

  • Date: 2020-02-28 & 29
  • Organizer: Association of Computer Engineering Students
  • Venue: University of Peradeniya
