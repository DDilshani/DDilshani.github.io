XbotiX 2018 Robotics Competition

The Xbotix Robotic Challenge - 2018 was held on 07th November 2018, which was organized by the EIES - Faculty of Engineering, University of Ruhuna. Our robot, Minotaur was able to select into final 6 teams from more than 35 teams.

  • Date: 2018-11-07
  • Organizer: Electrical and Information Engineering Society (EIES) and the Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering Students' Society (MMESS) in collaboration with the IEEE Student Branch and the IET Ruhuna Chapter
  • Venue: University of Ruhuna
