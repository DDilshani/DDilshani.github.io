Let me Hack v2.0

LetMeHack Eco 2K20 was the second product-oriented hackathon in the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka which was organized with the key idea of building up a completely open-source product that can be used by everyone.

Our team was able to obtain the 4th place among 20 finalist teams.

  • Date: 2020-02-01
  • Organizer: Sabaragamuwa University
  • Venue: Sabaragamuwa University
