Let me Hack v2.0

  • Date: 2020-02-01
  • Organizer: Sabaragamuwa University
  • Venue: Sabaragamuwa University

LetMeHack Eco 2K20 is the second product-oriented hackathon competition of the University of Sabaragamuwa in Sri Lanka. It was organized with the primary goal of developing an open-source product that can be used by anyone.

We won 4th place among 20 finalist teams.
