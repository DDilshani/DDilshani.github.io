Sri Lanka Robotics Challenge

SLRC is an all-island annual Robotics competition organized by the ENTC, University of Moratuwa. In this competition, we had to make two robots. The first one is mobile and it should able to collect coins on a line maze and follow the dashed line. The second robot is stationary and it should able to remove and clear the path for the mobile robot. Our robots, The Flying Dutchman and the Kraken was able to be a finalist of the competition.

  • Date: 2019-01-19
  • Organizer: ENTC, University of Moratuwa
  • Venue: University of Moratuwa
